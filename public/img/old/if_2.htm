We are Helping Hands

The Ark Help is a charity aimed at helping Those In Need. The charity will try to provide for those orphans, handicapped, sick and poor who are in a dire need of financial support.

The Ark Help will run from Jammu & Kashmir and will have a transparent running policy, meaning, whoever wishes to keep a check on how the donations are being spent, would be able to demand the income and expenditure report which would have to coincide with the bank's information about the charity's account.

The aim of the charity will be to keep an eye out for those who cannot afford the basic ammenities of life like food, shelter, clothing and education.
